  • WIN: Adds WIN rate to the shooter and subtracts the same from the previous player. Also updates the turn order (Guard logic).
  • FOUL+: Subtracts FOUL rate from the shooter and adds it to the previous player.
  • BC (Break & Clear): Subtracts BC from each opponent and adds the total to the shooter. (Enabled only for the current shooter.)
  • GOLDEN: Subtracts WIN from each opponent and adds the total to the shooter. (Enabled only for the current shooter.)
  • UNDO: Open History and tap Undo Last Action (or press z).
  • Tips: Click a score to set it manually. Toggle 3/4 players with the button at the top.
